Why Our Website Is Important

Professional and amateur athletes get injured every day. A recent study from the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services found that on average 8.6 million sports injuries occur each year. Athletes are constantly traveling so when they do get injured it is not easy to find a doctor. So, a lot of them just must work injured.



The NFL has some of the highest numbered injuries. 154 players get injured in the first half of the season and or preseason. Yet people are still expecting them to play so they usually still work. But if they had an easier and more accessible way to get the treatment, they need then less athletes would have to play injured.



So, our website will help athletes find doctors who specialize in the type of injury that you have. You just need to fill out this form and well pair you with a qualified doctor.
